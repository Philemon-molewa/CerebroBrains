Recipe Reader it is an AI virtual machine that reads recipes to the user based on the user specifications
and the type of the meal they want to prepare.
This machine can be used for personal use or in the hospitality industries.

*Benefits of Recipe Reader*
saves time - sometimes as a chef or a cook you do not have enough time to go through cookbooks or websites for recipes and
             and ingredients.
personalization - the AI can suggest recipes based on the users dietary preferences, allergies, and ingredients.
the can introduce users to new meals that they have not tried before.
meal planning - generate meal plans for the week including grocery lists and nutritional information.

*Some of the AI features*
suggestion of alternative ingredients 
step-by-step instructions, clear easy to follow cooking instructions
user feedback, by rating and reviewing recipes helping the AI improve its suggestions
